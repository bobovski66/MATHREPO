Z-Score Comparison Questions

  1. A student scored 98 on a History test with a mean of 79 and a standard deviation of 10. The same student scored 68 on a Economics test with a mean of 63 and a standard deviation of 9. Which test score is better relative to its class performance?

    Answer

    History z = 1.90, Economics z = 0.56. So, History score is better.

  2. A student scored 86 on a Math test with a mean of 66 and a standard deviation of 8. The same student scored 82 on a Physics test with a mean of 72 and a standard deviation of 5. Which test score is better relative to its class performance?

    Answer

    Math z = 2.50, Physics z = 2.00. So, Math score is better.
