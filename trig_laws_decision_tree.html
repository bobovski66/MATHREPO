Which Law Do I Use?

Goal Pick the right law based on what data are given.
Heuristic Opposite pair → Sines. SAS/SSS → Cosines.
Remember Bigger angle ↔ longer opposite side.

Formulas

Law of Sines

\[\frac{a}{\sin A}=\frac{b}{\sin B}=\frac{c}{\sin C}\]

Best for AAS/ASA or SSA (with care).

Law of Cosines

\[c^2=a^2+b^2-2ab\cos C\]

Rotate \(a,b,c\) and \(A,B,C\) cyclically for the other forms.

Common data patterns

ASA / AAS → Law of Sines
Two angles are known. Find \(C\) and use Sines.
SAS → Law of Cosines first
Two sides with included angle → Cosines, then Sines.
SSS → Law of Cosines
All sides are known → Cosines for one angle (largest first).
SSA (Ambiguous) → Law of Sines + check
SSA can give 0/1/2 triangles → Sines, then test the other possible angle.
